Privacy Policy

At Sree Nandanam Ayurveda Hospital, we are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of our patients and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, age, gender, and address
  • Medical Information: Health history, treatment details, reports, and prescriptions
  • Website Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and browsing behavior
  • Communication Data: Messages, appointment requests, and inquiries submitted through our website or contact channels

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Providing medical consultation and treatment
  • Scheduling appointments and patient management
  • Maintaining medical records and treatment history
  • Communicating important updates, follow-ups, and health advice
  • Improving our website, services, and patient experience

We ensure that your data is used only for legitimate healthcare and operational purposes.

Confidentiality of Medical Information

  • All patient medical records are kept strictly confidential.
  • Information is accessible only to authorized medical and administrative staff.
  • We do not disclose medical details without patient consent, except when required by law.

Data Protection & Security

  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.
  • Digital data is stored securely, and physical records are maintained with strict access control.
  • We regularly review our data protection practices to ensure safety and compliance.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Information may be shared only in the following cases:

  • With healthcare professionals involved in your treatment
  • When required by legal or regulatory authorities
  • With your explicit consent

Website Cookies & Tracking

  • Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic.
  • Cookies help us understand user preferences and improve website functionality.
  • Users can disable cookies through browser settings if preferred.

Third-Party Links

  • Our website may contain links to external websites.
  • We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites.
  • Users are advised to review the privacy policies of those websites separately.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal and medical information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent for certain uses of your data (where applicable)
  • Request deletion of data, subject to legal and medical record retention requirements

Data Retention

  • Medical records are retained as per applicable healthcare regulations and standards.
  • Personal data is stored only for as long as necessary for treatment and operational purposes.

Updates to This Policy

  • This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in legal, technical, or operational requirements.
  • Updates will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.
