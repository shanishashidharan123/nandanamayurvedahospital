Skin & Hair Treatment

Natural care for healthy skin and strong, beautiful hair

At Sree Nandanam Ayurveda Hospital, our Skin & Hair Treatment program focuses on healing from within. Instead of temporary cosmetic solutions, Ayurveda treats the root causes of skin and hair problems — such as toxin buildup, hormonal imbalance, poor digestion, stress, and dosha disturbances.

Through personalized therapies, herbal medicines, and lifestyle guidance, we help restore your natural glow, improve hair strength, and support long-term skin and scalp health.

How Ayurveda Treats Skin & Hair Disorders?

In Ayurveda, skin and hair health are closely connected to Pitta and Kapha imbalance, digestive weakness, and toxin accumulation (Ama). When these are disturbed, it can lead to acne, pigmentation, premature greying, dandruff, hair fall, and chronic skin conditions.

Our treatments aim to:

  • Purify the blood and detoxify the body
  • Balance hormones and digestion
  • Nourish skin and hair from the inside
  • Improve circulation to the scalp and skin
  • Reduce inflammation, itching, and dryness

Every patient is unique, so each treatment plan is fully personalized based on your body constitution (Prakriti), lifestyle, age, and the severity of your condition. This ensures long-lasting results, better mobility, and a healthier, pain-free life — naturally.

Who Can Benefit? (Skin & Hair Treatment)

This treatment is especially beneficial for those who suffer from recurring acne, pimples, pigmentation, dark circles, uneven skin tone, or premature ageing. If you experience frequent breakouts, excessive oiliness, dryness, itching, or sensitivity, these may be signs of internal toxin accumulation and dosha imbalance, which Ayurveda can correct.

People facing hair fall, thinning hair, dandruff, scalp infections, premature greying, or slow hair growth can also benefit greatly from this therapy. Stress, hormonal changes, poor digestion, lack of sleep, and pollution often weaken hair roots and damage the scalp. Ayurvedic treatments work at a deeper level to nourish hair follicles, improve blood circulation to the scalp, and restore natural strength and shine.

This program is suitable for students, working professionals, homemakers, and elderly individuals who wish to improve their appearance while also enhancing their overall health. It is also helpful for those with chronic skin conditions such as eczema, psoriasis, fungal infections, or allergic rashes, where long-term management is needed.

Even if you have tried creams, medications, or salon treatments with only short-term results, Ayurveda offers a holistic approach that treats the root cause. Our therapies are gentle, personalized, and designed to bring lasting balance, confidence, and wellness.

What to Expect During Your Treatment Stay

Your journey begins with a detailed Ayurvedic consultation to understand your skin or hair condition, body constitution, lifestyle, and diet habits. Based on this assessment, a personalized treatment plan is created.

You will receive daily external therapies such as oil massages, herbal packs, scalp treatments, or detox procedures, along with internal herbal medicines. Doctors monitor your progress regularly and adjust the plan if needed.

You will also receive simple diet and lifestyle guidance to support healing from within. By the end of the program, most patients notice clearer skin, healthier hair, improved confidence, and a sense of overall well-being.
