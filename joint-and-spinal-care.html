Joint & Spinal Care

Relieve pain. Restore movement. Rebuild strength – naturally.

At Sree Nandanam Ayurveda Hospital, our Joint & Spinal Care program is designed to treat the root cause of pain and stiffness rather than just suppressing symptoms. Using classical Ayurvedic therapies, herbal medicines, and personalized treatment plans, we help patients recover mobility, reduce inflammation, and regain confidence in daily movement.

This therapy is ideal for people suffering from chronic back pain, neck stiffness, frozen shoulder, knee pain, disc issues, arthritis, and post-injury weakness.

How Ayurveda Treats Joint & Spine Disorders?

According to Ayurveda, joint and spine disorders do not occur suddenly — they develop over time due to an imbalance in Vata dosha, poor digestion, toxin buildup (Ama), weakened tissues, and restricted circulation. When Vata becomes aggravated, it dries and stiffens the joints, compresses nerves, and causes pain, cracking sounds, numbness, and limited movement. At the same time, poor circulation and accumulated toxins block the body’s natural healing pathways, while weak muscles and bones fail to support the spine properly.

At Sree Nandanam Ayurveda Hospital, our treatments are designed to correct these imbalances at their root. Instead of masking pain, we focus on restoring the body’s natural balance and strengthening the system from within.

Our holistic approach works by:

  • Reducing inflammation and nerve compression through herbal oils, heat therapies, and detoxifying procedures that ease pain and swelling naturally.
  • Improving blood circulation so oxygen and nutrients reach damaged tissues, speeding up healing and relieving stiffness.
  • Nourishing muscles, joints, and spinal tissues with medicated oils and herbal formulations that rebuild strength and flexibility.
  • Strengthening the nervous system to improve coordination, reduce spasms, and calm pain signals.
  • Restoring flexibility, posture, and movement, allowing you to sit, stand, walk, and lift with comfort and confidence.

Every patient is unique, so each treatment plan is fully personalized based on your body constitution (Prakriti), lifestyle, age, and the severity of your condition. This ensures long-lasting results, better mobility, and a healthier, pain-free life — naturally.

Janu Vasti treatment

Janu Vasti is a classical Ayurvedic therapy specifically designed for knee joint care. In this treatment, a ring made of herbal dough is placed around the knee, and warm medicated oil is poured and retained inside it for a specific duration.

Janu Vasti is a non-invasive, natural, and highly effective treatment that focuses on healing the joint from within. It is especially beneficial for patients who want to avoid long-term painkillers or surgical procedures.

Key Therapies Used

  • Abhyanga (Herbal Oil Massage)

    Warm herbal bundles are applied to reduce pain, swelling, and stiffness.
  • Vasti (Medicated Enema Therapy)
    One of the most powerful therapies for spine, nerve, and joint disorders. It cleanses toxins and strengthens tissues.
  • Pizhichil & Dhara

  • Lepam & Local Treatments
    Herbal pastes applied to affected areas for targeted pain relief.

Who Can Benefit?

Joint & Spinal Care treatment at Sree Nandanam Ayurveda Hospital is suitable for people of all ages who are suffering from pain, stiffness, or restricted movement.

You can benefit if you have:

  • Chronic back, neck, or shoulder pain
  • Frozen shoulder or difficulty lifting the arms
  • Knee pain or difficulty walking
  • Slip disc, sciatica, or nerve compression
  • Cervical or lumbar spondylosis
  • Arthritis (osteoarthritis & rheumatoid)
  • Post-injury or post-surgery stiffness
  • Muscle weakness or poor posture
  • Stress-related body pain and fatigue

Even if you have tried other treatments with only temporary relief, Ayurvedic therapy can help address the root cause and support long-term healing.

What to Expect During Your Treatment Stay

At Sree Nandanam, every patient receives personalized care in a calm, healing environment.

  1. Initial Consultation
    Your treatment begins with a detailed Ayurvedic assessment to understand your body type, pain condition, lifestyle, and medical history.
  2. Customized Treatment Plan
    Based on the diagnosis, our doctors design a therapy program using oils, herbal medicines, and procedures tailored to your needs.
  3. Daily Therapies
    You will receive 1–2 therapies daily, such as Abhyanga, Kizhi, Vasti, or Dhara, performed by trained therapists.
  4. Internal Medicines
    Herbal medicines and decoctions are prescribed to support healing from within.
  5. Diet & Lifestyle Guidance
    Simple food plans and daily habits are suggested to reduce pain and prevent recurrence.
  6. Progress Monitoring
    Doctors regularly review your response and adjust treatments to ensure the best results.
