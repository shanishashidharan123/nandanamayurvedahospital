Cancellation & Refund Policy

At Sree Nandanam Ayurveda Hospital, we understand that plans may change. This policy outlines the terms for cancellation, rescheduling, and refunds to ensure transparency and smooth service for all patients.

Appointment Cancellation

  • Appointments can be cancelled or rescheduled by informing us in advance via phone or official communication channels.
  • We recommend cancelling at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled appointment time.
  • Late cancellations or no-shows may affect future booking availability.

Rescheduling Policy

  • Patients may reschedule appointments based on availability.
  • Requests for rescheduling should be made as early as possible.
  • The hospital will make reasonable efforts to accommodate preferred timings.

Consultation Fees

  • Consultation fees once paid are generally non-refundable.
  • In case of valid reasons and prior notice, rescheduling may be allowed instead of cancellation.

Treatment & Therapy Packages

  • Advance payments made for treatment packages may be partially refundable depending on the stage of treatment.
  • If treatment has already started, charges for completed sessions will be deducted.
  • Customized or specially prepared treatments/medications are non-refundable.

In-Patient (IP) Cancellation

  • For in-patient admissions, advance payments may be refunded if cancellation is made before admission.
  • If cancelled after admission, applicable charges for stay, consultation, and therapies will be deducted.

Refund Process

  • Eligible refunds will be processed within 7–14 working days.
  • Refunds will be made through the original mode of payment or as per hospital policy.
  • Any transaction or processing charges (if applicable) may be deducted.

Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be applicable in the following cases:

  • No-show for scheduled appointments
  • Last-minute cancellations without prior notice
  • Completed consultations or therapies
  • Purchase of medicines once dispensed
  • Personalized treatment plans already initiated

Emergency & Special Cases

  • Refunds or adjustments may be considered in genuine medical or emergency situations at the discretion of the hospital management.
  • Supporting documents may be required for such requests.

Changes to Policy

  • The hospital reserves the right to modify this policy at any time without prior notice.
  • Updated policies will be reflected on this page.
