Sree Nandanam Ayurveda Hospital is a trusted center for traditional healing, dedicated to restoring health through the timeless science of Ayurveda. Located at Kadavilla Junction, Alamcode, Attingal (near Ambady Stores), Thiruvananthapuram, the hospital has become a well-known destination for patients seeking safe, natural, and long-lasting solutions to chronic health conditions.

With a strong foundation in classical Ayurvedic principles and a compassionate approach to patient care, Sree Nandanam Ayurveda Hospital offers personalized treatment programs designed to address the root cause of disease rather than only the symptoms. The hospital is widely respected in the Alamcode and Attingal region for its successful management of thyroid disorders, diabetes, migraine, and other chronic illnesses through holistic therapies and herbal medicines.

A Center Rooted in Authentic Ayurveda

Ayurveda is a complete system of natural medicine that has been practiced for over 5,000 years. It is based on the balance of the three doshas—Vata, Pitta, and Kapha—which govern all physical and mental functions in the body. At Sree Nandanam, treatment begins with understanding each patient’s unique body constitution (Prakriti) and current imbalance (Vikriti). This personalized diagnosis ensures that every therapy, medicine, and lifestyle recommendation is specifically tailored for the individual.

The hospital strictly follows classical Ayurvedic texts while applying them in a practical and modern clinical environment. Only high-quality herbal formulations, traditional oils, and natural ingredients are used. Each medicine is carefully selected based on the patient’s age, digestion, disease condition, and overall health, making the treatments both gentle and effective.

Specialized in Chronic Disease Management

The hospital is particularly known for its success in treating thyroid disorders, diabetes, migraine, and lifestyle-related conditions. These diseases often require long-term care and careful management. At Sree Nandanam, treatment focuses on correcting metabolic imbalances, improving digestion, and strengthening the immune system, which helps achieve sustainable results.

In addition to these specializations, the hospital also treats:

  • Joint and spine disorders
  • Arthritis, back pain, and neck pain
  • Digestive and gastric issues
  • Skin and hair conditions
  • Stress, anxiety, and sleep disorders
  • Women’s health problems
  • Metabolic and hormonal imbalances

Each patient receives a customized treatment plan that may include medicines, therapies, diet guidance, and lifestyle correction.

Panchakarma and Therapeutic Care

Panchakarma is a powerful detoxification and rejuvenation therapy in Ayurveda. At Sree Nandanam, Panchakarma procedures are administered under medical supervision in a clean and peaceful environment. These therapies help remove toxins from the body, improve digestion, balance the doshas, and restore vitality.

The hospital also offers a range of specialized therapies such as:

  • Abhyanga (therapeutic oil massage)
  • Shirodhara
  • Kizhi therapies
  • Steam therapy
  • Herbal poultice treatments

These therapies not only relieve pain and stiffness but also promote relaxation, mental clarity, and emotional balance.

Healing Through Food and Lifestyle

At Sree Nandanam, food is considered a form of medicine. Patients receive simple, nourishing diet plans based on Ayurvedic principles. These diets are designed according to the patient’s condition, body type, and digestive strength. Along with food, patients are guided on proper daily routines, sleep habits, stress management, and gentle physical activities to support long-term health.
