Treatment & Therapy Policy

At Sree Nandanam Ayurveda Hospital, our treatments are rooted in authentic Ayurvedic principles while ensuring safety, hygiene, and personalized care. This policy outlines how therapies are planned, delivered, and monitored to provide the best possible healing outcomes.

Individualized Treatment Approach

  • All treatments are customized based on the patient’s Prakriti (body constitution), disease condition, age, and overall health status.
  • Treatment plans are designed only after detailed consultation with our Ayurvedic physicians.
  • No standard or “one-size-fits-all” therapies are followed.

Doctor-Prescribed Therapies

  • All therapies, including Panchakarma and other Ayurvedic procedures, are prescribed strictly by qualified doctors.
  • Therapies are carried out only under medical supervision.
  • Any modification in treatment is done based on patient response and clinical assessment.

Types of Therapies Offered

Depending on the condition, patients may undergo:

  • Panchakarma therapies – detoxification and rejuvenation procedures
  • Abhyanga – medicated oil massage
  • Shirodhara – continuous oil/herbal decoction therapy on the forehead
  • Njavarakizhi / Kizhi therapies – therapeutic sweating treatments
  • Vasti (medicated enema) – one of the key Panchakarma treatments
  • Specialized therapies for neurological, musculoskeletal, skin, and lifestyle disorders

All therapies are performed using classical Ayurvedic methods and protocols.

Duration & Treatment Schedule

  • Duration of therapies varies based on disease condition and individual response.
  • Typical treatment programs may range from 7 days to 28 days or more.
  • Patients must strictly adhere to the scheduled timing for therapies to ensure effectiveness.
  • Delays or interruptions may affect treatment outcomes.

Use of Medicines & Oils

  • Only authentic, high-quality Ayurvedic medicines and oils are used.
  • Medicines may be prepared in-house or sourced from trusted Ayurvedic manufacturers.
  • Internal medicines must be taken strictly as prescribed by the doctor.
  • Self-medication or use of external products during treatment is not advised.

Pre & Post Therapy Guidelines

Patients are required to follow specific guidelines before and after therapies:

Before Therapy:

  • Avoid heavy meals immediately before treatment
  • Inform the therapist about any discomfort or health changes
  • Be prepared as per instructions (fasting or light diet if advised)

After Therapy:

  • Take adequate rest
  • Follow dietary restrictions (Pathya)
  • Avoid exposure to cold, dust, or strenuous activities
  • Follow post-therapy instructions strictly for best results

Safety & Monitoring

  • Patient condition is regularly monitored by doctors during the treatment period.
  • Therapies are adjusted if any discomfort or adverse reaction occurs.
  • Emergency care protocols are in place if required.
  • Patients must immediately report any unusual symptoms.

Therapist Standards

  • All therapies are performed by trained and experienced therapists.
  • Male therapists attend male patients and female therapists attend female patients (unless otherwise required and consented).
  • Professional conduct, hygiene, and patient comfort are strictly maintained.

Contraindications & Restrictions

  • Certain therapies may not be suitable for specific conditions such as pregnancy, acute illness, infections, or severe systemic disorders.
  • The doctor reserves the right to modify or stop treatment if medically necessary.
  • Patients must disclose all health conditions before starting therapy.

Expected Outcomes

  • Ayurvedic treatments focus on gradual, long-term healing rather than instant relief.
  • Results may vary depending on the individual, condition severity, and adherence to treatment guidelines.
  • Patients are encouraged to follow lifestyle and dietary advice even after completion of therapy.

Patient Cooperation

For effective results, patients are expected to:

  • Follow all medical advice, therapy schedules, and dietary restrictions
  • Maintain discipline throughout the treatment period
  • Avoid habits that interfere with healing (e.g., smoking, alcohol, irregular routines)

Commitment to Authentic Ayurveda

We are dedicated to:

  • Preserving classical Ayurvedic treatment methods
  • Ensuring safe and ethical therapy practices
  • Providing holistic healing focused on body, mind, and well-being
