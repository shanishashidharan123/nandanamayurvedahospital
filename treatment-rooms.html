Treatment Rooms & In-Patient Stay

Sree Nandanam Ayurveda Hospital

Treatment Rooms

Our treatment rooms are specially designed to provide a peaceful, hygienic, and healing atmosphere for all Ayurvedic therapies. Each room is maintained with strict cleanliness standards and traditional comfort to help patients relax and heal deeply.

  • Calm, private therapy rooms
  • Separate male & female treatment areas
  • Traditional Ayurvedic therapy beds (Droni)
  • Warm oil therapy and steam facilities
  • Soft lighting and ventilation
  • Trained therapists for every session
  • Doctor supervision during therapies

These rooms are ideal for Panchakarma, detox therapies, joint & spine care, skin & hair treatments, digestive care, and lifestyle disease management.

In-Patient Accommodation

A Comfortable Healing Stay at Sree Nandanam Ayurveda Hospital

To make your healing journey peaceful and stress-free, we offer comfortable and hygienic in-patient rooms designed for rest, privacy, and recovery.
Each room supports the Ayurvedic healing process with natural ventilation, calm interiors, and easy access to treatment areas.

Whether you choose a budget-friendly ward or a private room, we ensure a clean, calm, and supportive environment for your stay.
